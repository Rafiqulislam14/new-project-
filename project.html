Summer Vacation

In Bangladesh, summer vacation for schools and colleges is typically scheduled between April and June, after the final exams. However, in 2024, the summer vacation was shortened by one week. The original plan was for it to end on July 2nd, but it was shortened to June 25th, with classes resuming on June 26th. This was done to recover learning losses due to the heatwave and other factors affecting classroom activities.

It’s known for its very long, sandy beachfront, stretching from Sea Beach in the north to Kolatoli Beach in the south. Aggameda Khyang monastery is home to bronze statues and centuries-old Buddhist manuscripts. South of town, the tropical rainforest of Himchari National Park has waterfalls and many birds. North, sea turtles breed on nearby Sonadia Island.

Bandarban

Bandarban, officially Bandarban Hill District, is a district in southeastern Bangladesh and a part of the Chittagong Division. It's the remotest and least populated district in Bangladesh, known for its mountainous terrain and rich tribal culture. The area is popular with tourists for its tallest peaks, trekking opportunities, and diverse tribal communities.
